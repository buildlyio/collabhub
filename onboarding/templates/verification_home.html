How It Works

1
Hash Generation: When a document is signed or certificate is issued, a unique SHA-256 hash is generated from the document content.
2
Tamper Detection: Any modification to the document will result in a different hash, immediately revealing tampering.
3
Public Verification: Anyone with the hash can verify the document's authenticity without needing to log in.
